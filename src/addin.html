Office Add-in Playground

You can use theOffice Add-in Playground site from a web browser to look through Office.js code samples, or to view shared snippets. But in order to run the code,. you will need to do so from within an Office application (Excel, Word, etc.), and you will need to have acquired the Add-in.

You can acquire the add-in in two ways:

  • Search for the "Office Add-in Playground" add-in in the Office Store website.
  • Search and insert the add-in straight from within an Office application, by choosing the Insert tab in the Ribbon, followed by the Store button in the Add-ins group.

    • Having acquired the Add-in, choose the My Add-ins button (also under the Insert tab in the ribbon), and select the add-in from the dialog that appears.

    From there on out, on newer builds of Office, you should see the add-in appear in your Home tab. Otherwise, continue to insert it from Insert==>My Add-ins
